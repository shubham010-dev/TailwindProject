Frequently Asked Questions

What is ToDesktop?

ToDesktop helps you convert your web application into a native desktop app with zero hassle. Perfect for Electron-based distribution without the pain.

How do I distribute my app?

You can easily distribute your desktop app using auto-updates, version control, and platform-specific builds (macOS, Windows, Linux) managed by ToDesktop.

Do I need coding experience?

Nope! ToDesktop handles the tech side for you. Just drop in your web URL and configure your settings visually.

Is it secure?

Absolutely. ToDesktop wraps your web app in a secure Electron shell with automatic updates and code signing built-in.

How much does it cost?

There are multiple pricing tiers based on your needs—from indie developers to large teams. Visit the pricing page on ToDesktop for more.

What about support?

You'll get priority support on paid plans including onboarding assistance, troubleshooting, and updates help.
